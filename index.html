MT. Dews in Mapúa-SCaRP

  • Serverless Data Architecture
  • Internet-of-Things
  • Amazon Web Services

I am part of a great team which handles sensor prototyping, web design, and data architecture for the SCaRP or Simulating Cascading Rainfall-triggered Landslide Hazards in the Philippines. It is a DOST-PCIEERD and UK NERC driven project (Led by Engr. Fibor J. Tan) and are collaborated with Mapúa University (PH) and University of East Anglia (UK). Along with it are several organizations with the same purpose and desire to help people such as MGB, PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, NWRB and WPF. The prospected output of the project is to create a data-driven early warning system for landslides in several locations in the Philippines. My desire to create something to help people led me to this amazing team and project which my 9-to-5 job cannot contain.
